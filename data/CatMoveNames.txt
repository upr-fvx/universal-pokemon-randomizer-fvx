Words used for category-based move name randomization.

It is possible to add/remove words, and even categories,
but each mentioned category must have at least one assigned name.
Categories must correspond to MoveCategory enumerator values found in pokemon/MoveCategory.java. 
If you don't know what that means, don't worry about it.
